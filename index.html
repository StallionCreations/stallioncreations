About

Stallion Creations is a full-service tech repair company that specializes in fixing phones, high-end headphones, installing automobile AV equipment, and much more.  

Drop us a line and let us fix what’s broken!

We at Stallion Creations are dedicated to cranking life back into your broken cars, phones, and electronics. It all started as a dream. Born and raised in Madison, Wisconsin. With a passion for cars and a love for electronics, Dwight launched his career as a student studying Mechanical Engineering at The University of Wisconsin Milwaukee. Matching his strong family values, goal to add value to others, and strong mechanical expertise, Stallion Creations was formed. Serving the greater Milwaukee and Madison area, we are confident Stallion Creations is the customization and repair shop for you.
